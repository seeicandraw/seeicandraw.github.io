About Us

Everyone can draw and make great art. It is a God-given innate skill and you just have to know how to unearth it with the basic principles. Once you have it, you can use any medium to produce great art. "See, I Can Draw" will help you do just that.

For All

Bring everyone and enjoy great fun lessons with Donald as you unearth your potential as an artist…

It is open to kids 5 years and up and adults 100 years and below!

Come on, grab your pencil, pen, stylus, mouse or anything you want to draw with…

Also make sure you wear a hat… any hat!

Donald writes: I love art! I grew up learning the basics of art from my mom. This skill matured through high school and eventually architecture school. I have used art professionally in the fields of architecture and urban design. However I always see the impact the art tips I give my kids, (and kids of family & friends) make - and I want to share with you and your family as well.

Donald Buaku is the Urban Design Studio Lead in Houston Public Works, a trained architect, city planner, and an accomplished pen & ink and watercolor illustrator. He also does graphic design, video & sound editing and produces and instructs in the theater arts.

“You can enrich your lives and those of others with the art you make, or even pursue a career in the fine and creative arts.”

Why?

“In this day and age, kids especially are glued to screens and losing precious time and the fine motor skills of art, perception and design - which are inherently in them. Learning the fundamentals of drawing and art will open up a whole paradigm and worldview to them and increase their academic and mental abilities and cognition while enhancing their social skills.” - Donald

Where & When?

You can come to my home studio or I can come to your place anywhere in the Houston area. Just let me know your preference and we can arrange. We can also do group lessons for siblings, buddies or families.

We can arrange a location and time for your lessons when you contact me.
