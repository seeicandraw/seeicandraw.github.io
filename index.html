About Us

Everyone can draw and make great art. It is a God-given innate skill and you just have to know how to unearth it with the basic principles. Once you have it, you can use any medium to produce great art. "See, I Can Draw" will help you do just that.
Grab your pencil, pen, and drawing pad and get ready.
ALSO MAKE SURE YOU WEAR A HAT.... ANY HAT!

Donald writes: I love art! I grew up learning the basics of art from my mom. This skill matured through high school and eventually architecture school. I have used art professionally in the fields of architecture and urban design. However I always see the impact the art tips I give my kids, (and kids of family & friends) make - and I want to share with you and your family as well.

Donald has 20 years of experience in architecture, urban design & city planning and is also an accomplished pen & ink and watercolor illustrator. He also instructs in graphic design, public speaking and the theater arts.

Value

Kids are glued to screens and are losing precious time and the fine motor skills of art, perception and design - which are inherently in them.


Learning the fundamentals of drawing and art will open a whole paradigm and worldview to them and increase their academic and mental abilities & cognition while enhancing their social skills. This is something AI cannot do for them.
